PLASTIC OCEAN AMBASSADORS


Plastic Oceans at JMSS, created by Plastic Oceans Australasia, aims to eliminate the usage of Single Use Plastics in JMSS with practical and realistic approaches, while also articulating a bold vision of the future by joining forces with other institutions in providing educational resources about the conservation, improvement and protection of our natural environment from plastic waste.
